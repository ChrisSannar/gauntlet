# ADR 0005: Difficulty-based pacing — finish early, earn the week off

## Status
Accepted (2026-07-06)

## Context
Chris can commit 60 hours/week. Fixed-scope weekly challenges mean speed is
rewardable: the incentive should be to finish well and fast, not to stretch work to
fill time.

## Decision
Each week is a **fixed-scope challenge**, not a fixed-time one. If the challenge passes
grading early, the remainder of the week is time off. Challenges are therefore sized
for difficulty (a strong performer finishes in ~5–6 days at a 60 hrs/week pace).

## Consequences
- Scope per week must be genuinely hard — an early finish should be an achievement,
  not the default.
- Grading can be requested any day of the week, not only at week's end.
