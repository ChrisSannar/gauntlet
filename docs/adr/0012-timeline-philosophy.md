# ADR 0012: Timeline philosophy — coverage over calendar

## Status
Accepted (2026-07-06)

## Context
Chris liked all four rapid-project candidates and prefers thoroughness over squeezing
into exactly 10 weeks: "10 weeks is more of an aim of a deadline." Pacing is
difficulty-based (ADR 0005); he manages his own time.

## Decision
- The Gauntlet is **11 weeks planned**: four rapid projects (weeks 1–7) + flagship
  phase (weeks 8–11).
- Every week has a **hard deadline** (midnight, day 7). Finishing a week's milestone
  early lets the next week start immediately — so the whole Gauntlet can compress; it
  cannot extend except by explicit re-planning.
- Content coverage wins over calendar: all four principle projects stay in.

## Consequences
- An early finisher graduates early; a struggler still faces every deadline.
- Weeks are rolling — week N+1 starts when Chris kicks it off, no fixed weekday.
