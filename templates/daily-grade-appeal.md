# Daily Grade Appeal — YYYY-MM-DD

An appeal challenges a factual or rubric-application error in one finalized report. It
does not reopen the evidence window or request a general second opinion.

## Report challenged

- Day: `<YYYY-MM-DD>`
- Report ID: `<immutable report ID>`
- Evidence bundle: `<path or bundle ID>`
- Original Delivery / Mastery / Overall: `<scores and letter>`

## Single disputed claim

Copy the exact claim, finding, or anchor selection being challenged.

## Error category

Choose one: `FACTUAL_ERROR`, `EVIDENCE_OMISSION`, `RUBRIC_MISAPPLICATION`, or
`ARITHMETIC_ERROR`.

## Frozen evidence

Cite the exact file, line, test result, screenshot, trace, or browser artifact already
present in the frozen evidence bundle. New work and post-cutoff evidence are ineligible.

## Why the evidence changes the finding

Explain the smallest correction warranted. Do not argue from effort, intent, hardship,
or dissatisfaction with the grade.

## Requested correction

State the specific finding or score that should change and the rubric anchor that
supports it.

## Attestation

`I confirm this appeal relies only on the original frozen evidence bundle and raises one factual or rubric-application error.`
